Bass Foundry

2016

As 1.Studio for Bass Foundry

logo and identity design for a duo of DJs/EDM Music producers based in Delhi

Brief

The clients needed a unique identity to establish their newly formed group. For this, they envisaged a mascot that would be loud and rather aggressive looking, in tune with the type of music that they created. It was important for this graphic to be easily printed on merchandise that the duo intended to sell/distribute as part of their regular gigs.

Moreover, the mascot needed to be something that could later be easily animated or programmed to LED panels that could be worn on the body or scaled up for visual effect on larger stages.

Sketches

The process started with refining the initial sketch made by the band management. Out of all the candidates considered, the one shown below was chosen as the final candidate.

These sketches were done on a Surface Book with a Surface Pen.
