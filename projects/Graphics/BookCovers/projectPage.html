Digital Book Covers

2016
As 1.Studio for Juggernaut Books

This project was commissioned by Juggernaut books for their mobile application. The brief was to keep the brand language of the parent company but make these titles stand out as individual eBooks/compilations. Also, it was necessary for the images to be legible at relatively small thumbnail sizes. All of the work listed below is not used in their current iteration of the marketplace. I've marked out the titles that are.
