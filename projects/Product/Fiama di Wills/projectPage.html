fiama di wills

2017

Group with Ruchita Rajan and Shobhana Sridhar

Competition entry for a packaging redesign competition hosted by Fiama Di Wills for their shower gel product

Concept

The Fiama Di Wills woman is a confident and independent person. She knows what she wants and she works hard for it. She likes to experiment and try out new things, to break from the mundane. The shower is a quiet space that starts and ends her daily routine. It rejuvenates and kickstarts her day, it calms and helps her unwind at the end of it. Showers are her me time. Keeping this in mind, our packaging form and graphics have water at the core. Water as the refresher, the source of life, the mighty river, the calm ocean

Packaging

The problem statement asked for a design that would:

- dispense about 5ml of shower gel since most people do not know how to use the correct amount of product
- be able to sit on the variety of surfaces that exist in the Indian bathroom or be able to be mounted in a case where a shelf is not available
- redefine the form without changing it drastically
stand out amidst competition on a store shelf

Design decisions

For our design we decided to keep the shape of the bottle close to the standard dimensions of a shower gel packaging and retain material properties but make the following changes:

- shape the bottle like a teardrop (or as close to it as possible) to keep with the concept of being close to water
- design the bottle with a flat bottom and hooked, rounded top that can be placed on any surface or hung from any available hook
- make the hook slightly elastic that would allow it to open and close somewhat like a keychain to enable a firm grip on wherever it is hung from
- design a pressable button like indent on the front of the bottle that holds just about 4ml of shower gel. this button when depressed, will dispense the right amount of liquid
